What is MindGlow?

MindGlow is a digital wellness platform designed to promote mental health and emotional well-being through personalized mindfulness and meditation experiences. Utilizing AI, the platform creates customized meditation and mindfulness routines that adapt to the user's emotional state, preferences, and progress over time. It's aimed at individuals seeking to improve their mental health, companies looking to enhance employee well-being, and wellness professionals seeking tools for their practice.
